About me

I'm a current PhD Student at the Kahlert School of Computing at University of Utah. I work with Dr. Ziad Al-Halah in the Computer Vision Group. Our work primarily focuses on innovating and improving upon computer vision problems. Personally, I work on the Audio-Visual Multimodal Learning branch of computer vision.

Before joining UofU, I was working as a Machine Learning Engineer at a startup. Prior to this I worked as a Data Scientist at the largest telecommunication company in Pakistan, PTCL. I completed my undergraduate in 2021 with Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering from COMSATS University Islamabad (CUI) (Sum Cum Laude).
